Outlined 3 types × 4 sizes × 4 states = 48

Heavy button. 테두리만 있는 보조 버튼. Secondary action.

Borderless 3 types × 4 sizes × 4 states = 48

Light button. 배경·테두리 없는 Tertiary 버튼.

IconOnly 6 types × 4 sizes × 3 states = 72

아이콘만 있는 정사각형 버튼. Loading 없음.

Text / IconText 12 text rows + IconText 4 combos

배경·테두리·패딩 없음. brand·primary·secondary·link × 3 sizes + IconText (icon+text+chevron 4 조합, no-bg / pill-bg 2행).

Icon / Emoji 6 types × 3 states

ButtonIconEmoji 세로형(13px / 11px 2사이즈) · ButtonEmoji 가로형(40 / 32) · ButtonIcon(32×32·no bg) · ButtonScrap(24×24).

Filter 3 types × 5 states

h=32px 고정 pill. off ↔ on Variable Mode 전환. FilterItem · FilterIcon(text) · FilterIcon(icon).

Floating / Special 4 types × 3 states

ButtonFloating(dark pill h40) · ButtonSearch(brand pill h40/32) · ButtonTop(circle 56px).
